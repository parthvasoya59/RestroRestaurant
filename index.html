THE RESTRO

RESTRO is a beautiful spot with ample room and seating for patrons. The service is incredible and the atmosphere is just beautiful. This is where you bring friends, business colleagues for breakfast,lunch or dinner.

Customers sit at tables, their orders are taken by a waiter, who brings the food when it is ready. After eating, the customers then pay the bill.
